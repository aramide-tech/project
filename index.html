About Us

Star Organic Farm is a company involved in trading and manufacturing a wide range of Organic Products and spices to the consumers all around the world. Our company started in 1988, at a period when the world needed a mix of African Indigenous agricultural products. We produce a wide variety of products from fresh and processed Nutritious cereals, Pulses, Spices and Condiments, Cooking Oils and other kinds of Oil, wheat and Wheat flour,Rice and so much more.
